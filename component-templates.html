Frequently Asked Questions

Common Questions

How do I purchase tickets?

All tickets are sold through our Ludus ticketing system. Simply select your show from our calendar, choose your seats, and complete your purchase online. Tickets can also be purchased at our box office during business hours.

Is the theater wheelchair accessible?

Yes! Beale Street Theater is fully accessible with wheelchair seating available in multiple sections. We also provide assistive listening devices and welcome service animals.

Do you offer group rates?

Yes! Groups of 10 or more qualify for special pricing. Contact our box office at (928) 555-SHOW to arrange group tickets for your organization, school, or event.
