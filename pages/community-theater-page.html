Audition Preparation Tips

Before You Audition

  • Choose a song that suits your voice type
  • Practice with a pianist if possible
  • Read the full audition notice carefully
  • Review conflict dates and rehearsal schedule
  • Register online before audition day

Day of Audition

  • Arrive 15 minutes early to warm up
  • Bring sheet music in the correct key
  • Wear comfortable clothing you can move in
  • Bring headshot and resume (if available)
  • Be yourself and have fun!
