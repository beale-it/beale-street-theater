Volunteer Opportunities

Become Part of a Creative Community

Step into the enchanting world of storytelling and performance by volunteering with Beale Street Theater. This community-driven theater is committed to bringing captivating productions to life, and your involvement can make a significant difference. As a volunteer with Beale Street Theater, you might find yourself working backstage, assisting with set design, or helping with front-of-house operations during performances. From assisting with art exhibitions to organizing community events, your contributions can help ignite the spark of creativity in individuals of all ages.
