Parking

Free street parking is available along Beale St, and there is an on-site parking lot adjacent to the theater.

Accessibility

The building, including entrances, restrooms, lounge, and backstage areas are all accessible with ramps or flat surfaces. Seating marked with a wheelchair icon is reserved for patrons in wheelchairs or walkers with seats.
