ADOPT A SEAT

For a $1,000 donation, you can place a personalized brass plaque on a theater seat. Your name, a loved one's name, or a memorial message will be permanently displayed for a 10-year term.

This program allows a new generation of donors to support the theater each decade while honoring those who helped build our community's cultural foundation.

Your donation includes:

  • Personalized brass plaque (10-year term)
  • Recognition in donor wall display
  • Website listing as a seat sponsor
  • Certificate of appreciation
