Pay It Forward Tickets

Making Live Theater Accessible to All

Donate unused tickets to make live theater accessible to underserved youth and families. The Pay-It-Forward Ticket Donation Program at Beale Street Theater allows you to donate unused tickets, making live theater accessible to underserved youth and families. By donating tickets, you will share the joy of live performances with those who might not otherwise have the chance to attend. You or your business can get involved by purchasing tickets to benefit local families. If you need to donate unused tickets, contact the theater to arrange for ticket donations.
