Frequently Asked Questions

Ticket Information

How do I purchase tickets?

All tickets are sold through our Ludus ticketing system. Simply select your show from our calendar, choose your seats, and complete your purchase online. Tickets can also be purchased at our box office during business hours.

What is your refund policy?

Tickets are non-refundable but may be exchanged for another performance of the same show (subject to availability) up to 24 hours before showtime.

Do you offer group rates?

Yes! Groups of 10 or more qualify for special pricing. Contact our box office at (928) 228-5817 to arrange group tickets.
