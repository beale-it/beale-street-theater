Frequently Asked Questions

How do I purchase tickets?

All tickets are sold through our Ludus ticketing system online or at the box office.

Is the theater wheelchair accessible?

Yes, we are fully accessible with wheelchair seating and assistive listening devices available.

Do you offer group rates?

Groups of 10 or more qualify for special pricing. Contact the box office directly.

Theater Policies

Late Seating Policy

Latecomers will be seated at the discretion of the House Manager during an appropriate break in the performance.

Refunds & Exchanges

All sales are final. Exchanges can be made for another performance of the same show with 24 hours notice.

Outside Food & Drink

Outside food and beverages are not permitted inside the venue. Concessions are available in the lobby.
