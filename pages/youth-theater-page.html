Audition Preparation

  • Choose a song that showcases your voice
  • Practice your audition material multiple times
  • Arrive 15 minutes early to check in
  • Wear comfortable clothes you can move in
  • Have fun and be yourself!

What to Expect

Youth auditions are designed to be a supportive, encouraging experience. You'll perform your prepared song for the director and may be asked to read from the script or learn a short dance combination.

Callbacks will be posted within 48 hours. Not cast? We encourage you to audition again for future productions!
