Celebrating a Decade of Magic: The Beale Street Theater 10-Year Anniversary Gala

March 27 and March 28, 2026

For ten years, the heart of downtown Kingman has beaten in time with the music, laughter, and drama unfolding on our stage. This milestone is more than just an anniversary; it is a love letter to the stage and a celebration of the sweat, passion, and standing ovations that have defined us. We are honored to mark a decade of unforgettable performances, artistic excellence, and shared memories that have woven the Beale Street Theater into the very fabric of our community.

A Trip Down Memory Lane

The Showcase

To honor our journey, the Beale Street Theater Cast & Alumni are returning for a dynamic showcase that serves as a living retrospective of our history. This isn't just a show; it’s a breathtaking look back at the stories that moved us and the characters who became our friends. The performance will feature:

  • Musical Highlights: Show-stopping vocal and orchestral favorites from the past decade.
  • Iconic Scenes: Dramatic and comedic reprises of the most memorable moments from our stage.
  • A Community Retrospective: A curated visual journey featuring photos and videos contributed by you—our cast, crew, and fans—bringing ten years of backstage and onstage magic to life.

Help Us Tell Our Story

Community Call to Action

Our story has never been ours alone; it belongs to every performer who stepped into the spotlight, every crew member who worked in the wings, and every audience member who cheered from the house. To make our gala truly special, we need your help to fill our visual retrospective with the spirit of the last decade.

How to Contribute

  1. Search Your Archives: We are looking for your favorite behind-the-scenes snapshots, onstage photos, or video clips from any BST production over the last 10 years.
  2. Submit via Email: Send your digital files directly to info@bealestreettheater.com.
  3. Tag on Social Media: Share your favorite memories publicly using the hashtag #BealeStreet10.

While we look back at our first decade through your lens, we also invite you to help us build our next one. We are already looking toward the future, and you can explore our 2026 volunteer opportunities to see how you can be part of our next act.
